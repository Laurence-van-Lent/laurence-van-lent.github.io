Laurence van Lent

Professor of Accounting and Economics

Frankfurt School of Finance and Management

My research applies text-as-data methods to measure firms' exposure to political risk, climate change, and epidemic diseases, with work published in the Quarterly Journal of Economics, the Journal of Finance, the Review of Financial Studies, and the Journal of Economic Perspectives. A second research line studies organizational design, incentive contracts, and performance measurement, published in Management Science, the Journal of Accounting Research, The Accounting Review, and the Review of Accounting Studies.
